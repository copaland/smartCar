### 1.KiCad 설치 : 회로도 작성, PCB 설계
### 2.Arduino 설치 : Firmware 프로그래밍
### 3.MIT APP Inventor

### ESP-WROOM-02 PIN MAP
1-A0     SENS  
2-D1   5 TRIG  
3-D2   4 ECHO  
4-D3   0 LEFT SENS(FLASH)   
5-D4   2 RIGHT SENS(SDA)  
6-D5  14 AIN(SCL)  
7-D6  12 APWM   
8-D7  13 BPWM   
9-D8  15 BIN    
10-D9  3 RX  BT  
11-D10 1 TX  BT
13-D0 16 LED (Default)
